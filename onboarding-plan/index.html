1 Day 1

Welcome to your Onboarding

    We’re thrilled to have you rejoin the team! Over the next month, a dedicated team will support your reboarding, guiding you through the latest tools, processes, and updates.

    We’re excited to see the impact of your expertise and fresh perspective—let’s make this next chapter a success together!

Meet your Manager and Training Team

Schedule

  • Training will be conducted from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm.
  • Training will be paused during Holidays.
  • Our Mode of Training is Hybrid, where we will have both online and hands-on training.
  • We will have a break for lunch from 12:00 pm to 1:00 pm, and a 10 minute break every 2 hours.

2 First Week

Goals for the first week

  • Learn about the Tools GHD uses.
  • You will have your hardware training and learn about the GHD Website.
  • ServiceNow will be reintroduced to refresh your knowledge.
  • KCS and Technical Training will start.

3 First 15 / 30 / 60 Days

15 days

Build foundations: know your team, tools, and main processes. Complete any remaining training both technical and online training.

30 days

  • We aim to expose you to GHD Support starting from supporting Email, then Chat, and lastly Phone support.
  • A mentor will be assigned to help you with your learning journey.
  • Aside from your TL/Manager catch up. A Mentor catch up will be scheduled to ensure you are on track and answer any questions you may have.

60 days

Operate independently on core tasks. Have a 60-day review with your manager to align on next goals.
